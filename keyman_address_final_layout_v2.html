About Us

This home was built by my parents who believed deeply in progress and unity. My father was a deep thinker and teacher who guided people with wisdom and brought communities together. My mother was an unstoppable businesswoman, resilient and resourceful, who ensured growth and progress in everything she touched.

Today, Keyman Address carries forward their spirit. It is a place where couples rediscover love, families reconnect, small groups meet with purpose, and every guest leaves with both memories and the warmth of true hospitality.

About Food

At Keyman Address, food is part of the experience.

  • Cook for Yourself — Guests are welcome to use our indoor kitchen and open‑air garden kitchen.
  • Chef on Request — We can arrange for a private chef if you’d prefer meals prepared for you.
  • Order In — We can help you order from nearby Nyeri favorites: Tumu’s (steamed chicken), To Go Is To See (chicken with potatoes & bananas), Mombasa Ndogo – Giakanja (nyama choma), and Nyokabi’s (nyama choma).
  • Kitchen Garden — Guests can freely pick fresh vegetables and herbs straight from our garden during their stay.

Nyeri’s famous nyama choma — one of the many local delights we can organize for you.

House Rules

  1. Maximum Capacity: Events or gatherings are limited to 10 people. For more than 10 guests, prior approval is required via keymanaddress@gmail.com or Evelyn: 0729 917 462.
  2. Guest Responsibility: The booking guest is fully responsible for the behavior, safety, and actions of their guests. Any damages, breakages, or losses will be charged to the booking guest.
  3. Security Fee: For gatherings above 10 people (with approval), a KES 2,000 fee will be charged to cover security chaperone and gate services.
  4. Conduct: Drugs and loud, wild parties are not allowed on the premises.
  5. Quiet Hours: Please observe quiet time from 10:00 PM – 7:00 AM.
  6. Check‑In & Check‑Out: Check‑in from 2:00 PM, Check‑out by 11:00 AM. Late check‑out must be communicated in good time and confirmed by management. During check‑in, guests MUST fill in their details via the Check‑In button on this site, and also Check Out when leaving.
  7. CCTV: For your safety, CCTV cameras operate outdoors only. No cameras inside the house.
  8. Respect for Property: Treat the home, garden, and facilities with care so we keep the space serene and welcoming for everyone.
